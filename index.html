Indulge in refined elegance, savor unforgettable flavors, and unwind in pure bliss. Your journey to paradise begins here. Welcome to Island – where every moment is extraordinary.

OUR SELECTIONS OF ROOMS

Escape to serenity in our thoughtfully designed rooms at The Island. Each space blends modern luxury with coastal charm, offering plush bedding, panoramic views, and bespoke amenities. Whether you choose a breezy Ocean Suite or a cozy Garden Room, comfort and elegance await.

OUR SPA OFFER

Indulge in pure relaxation at The Island Spa, where tranquility meets rejuvenation. Our expert therapists use organic, locally inspired treatments to melt away stress—from tropical massages to revitalizing facials. Leave refreshed, inside and out.

OUR RESTAURANT SERVICES

Savor the flavors of the sea and sun at The Island’s signature restaurant. Our chefs craft seasonal dishes with the freshest local ingredients, paired with hand-selected wines. Dine under the stars or enjoy oceanfront breakfasts—every meal is a celebration.
